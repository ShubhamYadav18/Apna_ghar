About Us

A legacy of trust, built over two decades.

Our Story

Dr. Munshilal Yadav - Builder & Land Developers was founded with a singular vision: to make property ownership transparent, accessible, and hassle-free for everyone. With over 20+ years of experience in the real estate sector of Naigaon, Vasai Road, and Chinchoti, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the community.

Family-Run Business

We take pride in being a family-run business. The foundation was laid by Dr. Munshilal Yadav, whose commitment to integrity and community service set the standard for our operations.

Today, the legacy is carried forward by his son, Drx. Abhishek Yadav, who brings fresh energy and modern approaches while maintaining the core values of trust and transparency that our clients rely on.
